Julio Sala

Game Developer

I'm Julio Sala, a game programmer from Spain, and I've been creating games for 4 years. I'm finishing my degree in Multimedia Engineering. Here you can see some of the projects I have worked on or been a part of.

I have great perseverance in learning new technologies, and I've always had a strong curiosity about the technical aspects of games. Thanks to this, I have developed a Graphics Engine from scratch and made games in Assembly language.

I also have experience creating websites, like this one. If you have any questions, feel free to contact me! :)
